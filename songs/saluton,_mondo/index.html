saluton, mondo 1.0.0

this is our first song. it was released on 2017-12-21. it is licensed as cc by-sa. it is made only with free/libre/open-source software. the lyrics are in esperanto. isrc: be1gw1700001.

theme

this song is an ode to the beauty of the world and of life. ;)
